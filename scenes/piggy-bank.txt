# Piggy Bank

What... what is going on here? The walls are shaking, that horrible sound! Light! There's light! Ah, finally! Freedom! Mathilde has freed me from being trapped in the piggy bank's entrails! I can start my journey! Space is where I belong! Space is where I can finally shine!
